Solution: Contact Form

This contact form demonstrates a complete serverless communication pipeline. When you submit your message, it's processed through AWS Lambda functions, stored securely in DynamoDB, and automatically sends an email notification.

Frontend → Lambda API → DynamoDB → SES Email → Configured Recipient

Local Development Mode
Contact form is disabled in local environment. Deploy to AWS to enable email functionality.
